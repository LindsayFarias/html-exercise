Where I've Been:

I've lived my whole life in Texas, from growing up in small town Brenham, TX to attending college at Texas State University in San Marcos, TX. While attending college I jumped from apartment to apartment, living in a vairety of different towns. My senior year of college I decided to join my college's Air Force ROTC program, and from there I got sent to my first duty station at Patrick Space Force Base in Satellite Beach, FL. The journey is to continue soon as I am slotted to move to my next base June of 2022, destination unknown (for now)!

  • Texas
    • Brenham
      • I lived in Brenham from the ages 0-19. A great place to grow up! I miss the good barbeque and Blue Bell ice cream.
    • San Marcos
      • I lived in San Marcos from the ages 19-21. I attended college in here. I lived on campus and then in an apartment with a couple of friends.
    • Austin
      • I lived here for about six months with my then boyfriend while I found a place to live. I wish I could have stayed longer, by far my most favorit place. Within walking distance from the house is where I had the best jalapeno margaritas I've ever had in my whole entire life!
    • New Braunfels
      • I lived here for the remainder of my college years from the ages 21-23. This is a huge German town, they had great food and a hell of an Octoberfest!
  • Florida
    • Satellite Beach
      • This is where I have resided for the past two years and counting. I live right by the beach and have enjoyed the sunning it and bumming it lifesyle!
